Our Heritage

Founded in 1963 by Karl Toosbuy, ECCO was built on the philosophy that the shoe must follow the foot.

From our headquarters in Bredebro, Denmark, we have remained one of the few major shoe manufacturers in the world to own and manage every step of the leather-making and shoe-manufacturing process. Our heritage is rooted in the belief that true luxury is found in the intersection of traditional craftsmanship and modern material science.
